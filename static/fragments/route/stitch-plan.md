# Route: Stitch Plan

Use for paper ideas, A+B modules, thesis topics, opening reports, advisor plans, and experiment roadmaps.

## Required Moves

1. Identify the inherited baseline/task/paper family.
2. Name the exact failure mode that remains.
3. Map each candidate delta to a mechanism and measurable evidence.
4. Drop modules that lack attribution, compatibility, or testability.
5. Translate the surviving idea into work packages.

## Output Emphasis

- Paper matrix fields.
- Keep/drop decisions.
- Minimum viable research product.
- Experiment and writing checkpoints.
- Advisor-facing next decision.
