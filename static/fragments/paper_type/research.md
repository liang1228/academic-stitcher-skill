# Paper Type: Research

Use for primary findings, empirical studies, mechanisms, systems, or applied research.

## Reader Question

What was found, why does it matter, and what evidence makes it trustworthy?

## Argument Chain

Field pressure -> unresolved gap -> approach -> evidence -> interpretation -> boundary.

## Risks

- Overstating mechanism without direct evidence.
- Hiding negative or unstable results.
- Writing a method story when the evidence only supports a bounded empirical claim.
